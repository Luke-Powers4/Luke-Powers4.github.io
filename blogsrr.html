Risk of Rain Review Review  

by Luke Powers  

Review: https://www.codewithmike.com/risk-of-rain-2-review/ This review on risk of rain 2 has a lot of takes that I agree with but only really one thing I don't. He mentions how great the soundtrack is and I have to agree, I still listen to the soundtrack and I have multiple songs on my playlist. Another thing he mentions is the replayability with tons of different item combinations and I agree with this statement too. He mentions the variety in the classes and how each of them feel

different and fun in their own way. This is one of my favorite parts of Risk of Rain 2. Playing a different character is a big refresher and they are just fun to play. One thing he says that I don't agree with is that some of the hidden areas are a bad thing. I feel they bring mystery when you find them and it's cool to find them for the first time. Also he says they are hard to find and I feel they are easy to find. In conclusion i for the most part agree with this review but that one part i just mention i don't really agree with
