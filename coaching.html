Mechanics of the game

1. The way to win a match changes with game modes but I'm just going to talk about capture the point. The goal of capture the point is to be in your teams point radius for as long as possible and stop the other team from doing the same.

2. If your a healer a good strategy is to priorities healers then tanks then everyone else.

3. Overwatch is pretty easy to pick up and has a high skill ceiling depending on the character.
