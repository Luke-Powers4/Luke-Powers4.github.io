 Cloud 9 team analysis

by Luke Powers  

Cloud 9 is one of my favorite LOL esports teams. Cloud 9 established 8 divisions in 2014 and won three international championships in 2018. What makes cloud 9 so good is their petal to the metal gameplay to overwhelm the opposing team,

as well as their willingness to not underestimating opponents makes them a force to be reckoned with. One thing cloud 9 needs to work on is cleaner, tighter and consistent gameplay.
